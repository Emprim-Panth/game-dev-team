Complete game development knowledge base covering:

- **Development** - Godot 4.x implementation expertise
- **Balance** - Combat math, difficulty tuning, QA
- **Audio** - Music composition and sound design

**Built during:** Development of Oasis (mobile roguelike dungeon crawler)
